Introduction
Mickey mouse is an NFT collection of 10k items. This collection was made to help those in need and build a world wide brand. The items consist of a face , background, hat , jacket , eyes , short , an ethereum symbol and gloves. The main purpose of this project is to give back to the community.

Why Mickey
Our NFT collection is based on Mickey mouse a character , from disney. Mickey is a friendly and unselfish mouse who thinks and help others before himself. This coincides with our goal which is to help those in need so that they can have better lives like we do.Mickey is a leader and in participating in this project we all are leaders and alpha's of our generation.Growing up most of us watched the show and some of us still watch it.The show brings laughs and family adventures to all.

Why now
In Africa there are about 32 million orphans and very little orphanages to cater for all of them. Many of these kids have suffered all of their lives.These orphanages hardly have basic goods for all these kids due to lack of donations. We as a brand have found it worthwhile to make this project with over half of the profits going to orphans. The other half will be invested read utilities and roadmap for more information. These kids deserve a better life where they can learn , eat and sleep like we do.They are our future doctors, our future engineers and presidents. It all starts with you and me .Lets be an example to the rich people and build a better tommorrow with the little we have. We are all the mickey's of today, the leaders of today .

Future plans
This is just the start of something big . We look forward to building more NFT collection based on character of disney shows. This year we hope to launch 2 more projects before November.

Goals
Our primary goal is to help the kids have better future by building them farms and shops of which they can work on and have a salary. Our secondary goal which is equally important is to build a World Wide Brand where anyone can participate and earn an income.You ,the community will be shareholders of the future busimesses.For more read on roadmap and utilities and dont be left out.
